* Minimum qualifying mark in the written test is 40% for both Unit A and Unit B separately.

* Deduction of 5% marks will be made from the written test for the candidates passed HSC in 2022.

Merit list will be prepared based on:

  • Total marks obtained in Written Test - 60%
  • Total marks obtained in Higher Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry in HSC/ equivalent - 20%
  • Total marks obtained in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry in SSC/ equivalent - 20%
